System Requirements

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit), macOS 10.15+, or modern Linux
  • CPU: Quad-core processor (e.g., Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 from ~2018 or later)
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Storage: 10 GB of free space
  • GPU: Not required

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit), macOS 12+, or modern Linux
  • CPU: 6-core or 8-core processor (e.g., Intel Core i7 or AMD Ryzen 7 from ~2020 or later)
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • Storage: 20 GB of free space on an SSD
  • GPU: NVIDIA GPU with CUDA support (e.g., GeForce GTX 1660 or better) with at least 6 GB of VRAM

Ultimate

  • OS: Windows 11, macOS 13+, or modern Linux
  • CPU: High-end, multi-core processor (e.g., Intel Core i9 or AMD Ryzen 9)
  • RAM: 32 GB or more
  • Storage: 50 GB of free space on a fast NVMe SSD
  • GPU: High-end NVIDIA RTX GPU (e.g., GeForce RTX 3080 or better) with 10 GB+ of VRAM
